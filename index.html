About Our Barangay

Barangay San Isidro is a vibrant community with approximately 8,500 residents. We strive to deliver quality basic services, maintain peace and order, promote sustainable development, and preserve our Filipino values and traditions.

Our Vision

A progressive, peaceful, and God-loving barangay where every resident lives with dignity and opportunity.

Our Mission

To provide efficient, transparent, and responsive governance for the general welfare of all constituents.
