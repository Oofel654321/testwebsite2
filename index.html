Tungkol sa Aplaya

Ang Barangay Aplaya ay nakatuon sa malinaw at tapat na pamamahala, mahusay na serbisyong pampubliko, at sa pagtataguyod ng isang progresibo, mapayapa, at nagkakaisang komunidad

Our Vision

A progressive, peaceful, and God-loving barangay where every resident lives with dignity and opportunity.

Our Mission

To provide efficient, transparent, and responsive governance for the general welfare of all constituents.
